What is Web-Technologies?

"Web Technologies 1 - Frontend" is a 10-week course covering the basics of web development with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. Learn to design functional, responsive websites.

Course Policies

  • Attendance Requirement:
    Students must maintain a minimum attendance of 70% to qualify for final examinations.
  • Attestation 1 and 2 Requirements:
    Students must achieve at least 25% in both Attestation 1 and Attestation 2 to pass.
  • Final Exam Requirements:
    A score of at least 50% is required to pass the final exam.
